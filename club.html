How It All Started

It began, as many great things do, with a disagreement about carbonara. Three friends — Giulia, Marco, and Simone — were debating the correct use of cream in pasta recipes (spoiler: there is none) when they realized the world needed a dedicated space for people who truly, deeply care about pasta.

PastaLoverClub launched in 2019 as a small Instagram page and a shared Google Doc of family recipes. By 2021, we had grown into a community of over 5,000 members across 30 countries. Today, we're 10,000 strong — sharing recipes, debating shapes, and fiercely defending regional traditions.

Our mission is simple: celebrate pasta in all its forms. From a Tuesday night spaghetti aglio e olio to a Sunday ragù that simmers for six hours, every bowl deserves respect.

Our Mission

Pasta is for everyone.

We believe in the democratizing power of a perfect plate of pasta. It doesn't matter if you're a Michelin-starred chef or someone who just mastered boiling water — if you love pasta, you belong here. We curate recipes, foster community, and occasionally argue about whether rigatoni is superior to penne (it is).
